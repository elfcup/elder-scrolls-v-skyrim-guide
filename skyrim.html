Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

Beginners Information Guide

Welcome to our dedicated Skyrim beginners guide! This page is dedicated to all Skyrim fans, and gamers. As faithful fans of the Elder Scrolls series we put together a brief guide that will enhance your Syrim experience. Below, you will find a few tools and resources that we believe will help you through your Skyrim journey. We hope these resources are beneficial to you, and wish you luck on your journey!

Name Generator and Character detail: In this section, you can select a characters race, class, and gender. Each combination will provide a brief a suggestion of names, an image of how your character could look, and a brief summary of strengths and weaknesses.

Skyrim Wiki: Skyrim is one of the larger Elder Scrolls game. Our site doesn't come close to containing all the resources needed to navigate this massive world, so the inclusion of a Wiki will be helpful for all! We encourage you to lookup any topic we have not covered.

Skyrim Map and Resources: The Skyrim universe is enormous. We have provided a map of the Skyrim world that depicts each primary region, and a few helpful resources for each.
